Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

Introduction​

This privacy policy applies to Spacerivers, a construction workforce management application developed by Spacerivers.

Spacerivers helps construction companies manage employee attendance, work records, and site documentation. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information when using the application.

Permissions We Use​

Location​

  • Location is captured only during clock-in and clock-out events
  • Used to verify employee presence at construction sites
  • Location is not tracked continuously or in the background

Camera​

  • The app uses the device camera during clock-in and clock-out
  • Photos are used for attendance verification
  • May also capture site documentation photos

Storage​

  • Used to store photos and application data temporarily
  • Allows offline usage when internet is unavailable
  • Data automatically syncs when connection is restored

Internet​

  • Required to synchronize attendance records with backend servers
  • Uploads photos and work records securely
  • Enables real-time updates for management and supervisors

Data We Collect​

Employee Information​

  • Employee name and employee ID
  • Clock-in and clock-out timestamps
  • GPS location captured during attendance
  • Attendance verification photos
  • Site documentation photos

Project Information​

  • Construction site locations
  • Work assignments and activity records
  • Project documentation data

Data Sharing​

We share collected data only with authorized parties related to the project:

  • Employer or company management
  • Project managers and supervisors
  • Human Resources (HR) department
  • Payroll department for salary processing

Spacerivers does not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

Data Security​

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.

All communication between the mobile application and our servers is transmitted using secure encrypted connections. Access to stored data is limited to authorized personnel only.

Your Rights​

Users have the right to:

  • View their attendance records
  • Request corrections to incorrect data
  • Access personal information stored in the system
  • Control app permissions through device settings
  • Request data deletion after employment ends

Legal​

By using the Spacerivers application, you consent to:

  • Location capture during clock-in and clock-out
  • Photo capture for attendance verification
  • Data sharing with your employer for operational purposes
  • Secure storage and processing of attendance records as required by business and legal obligations
